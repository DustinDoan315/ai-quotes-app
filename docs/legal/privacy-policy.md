# Inkly: Daily Vibes Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026

Inkly: Daily Vibes ("Inkly", "we", "our", or "us") helps users create personal quote moments from photos, profile preferences, and daily app activity. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use Inkly:

- Account information, such as your user ID, display name, avatar, and sign-in provider when you choose to sign in.
- Guest profile information, such as local persona choices, quote language, reminder preferences, and display name.
- User content, such as photos you choose to capture or upload, generated quotes, memories, reactions, messages, and invite/friend activity.
- App usage information, such as quote generation counts, paywall events, purchase state, streaks, reminders, app diagnostics, and crash reports.
- Device and technical information, such as app version, device platform, notification permission state, and network request metadata.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Generate and personalize quotes.
- Save and display quote memories.
- Support friend invitations and social quote sharing.
- Manage subscriptions, paywall access, restore purchases, and free usage limits.
- Send reminders when you enable notifications.
- Improve app reliability, debug issues, prevent abuse, and understand app usage.

## Photos and AI Processing

When you ask Inkly to generate a quote from a photo, the photo or processed image data may be sent to our backend and AI service providers to analyze the image and generate a quote. Do not upload photos that you do not have permission to use or that contain sensitive information you do not want processed.

## Third-Party Services

Inkly may use third-party services for:

- Authentication and data storage, including Supabase.
- AI processing, including OpenAI.
- Subscription and purchase management, including RevenueCat and Apple App Store services.
- Analytics, diagnostics, and crash reporting, such as PostHog and Sentry when configured.

These services may process information according to their own privacy practices.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information:

- With service providers needed to operate the app.
- When you choose to share content or invite friends.
- To comply with law, enforce our terms, prevent fraud or abuse, or protect rights and safety.
- In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

## Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may delete local app data by uninstalling the app. If you have an account and want account data deleted, contact us using the information below.

## Notifications

Inkly sends reminders only if you enable notifications. You can disable notifications in the app settings or your device settings.

## Children

Inkly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Continue as a guest without signing in.
- Change quote language and reminder settings in the app.
- Disable notifications through your device settings.
- Restore or manage subscriptions through your Apple App Store account.
- Contact us to request help with account data access or deletion.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
